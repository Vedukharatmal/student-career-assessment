About Student Career Assessment

Our platform helps students discover their strengths and interests by providing carefully designed career assessments across fields like Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering.

We believe that every student deserves guidance when choosing a career path. By answering simple interest-based questions, you receive personalized suggestions that align with your passions.

This project was built with modern web technologies to ensure a smooth experience for students and institutions alike.
